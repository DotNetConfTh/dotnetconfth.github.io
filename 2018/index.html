About the event

Join us for .NET Conf, an event where we will be showcasing many of the advancements and capabilities of .NET. including some of the cool technologies that were recently announced by Microsoft. These technologies (but not limited to) include Web, Mobile Desktop, .NET Core, EF Core, C#, IoT, AI, ML, and Mobile.

.NET Conf is partnering with organizers around the globe to bring you 158 local in-person events between September 12 through the end of October! Join your fellow developers in a city near you to learn more about .NET.
